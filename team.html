Meet the Team

Mohammad Omeiza Aliyu
Co-Founder, Head of Operations
Mohammad Omeiza Aliyu is a Civil Engineer with an extensive decade long career. During which he has worked within different capacities to serve governments and organizations such as the Kogi state government, the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), SOS Children’s Villages International and WORLD BANK.
Mohammad attained his bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering from Ahmadu Bello University Zaria, and then went on to obtain a training certificate from Queensland University of Technology in Australia, He is currently attending Ahmadu Bello University to gain a master's degree in Civil Engineering. He is a member of COREN, NSE and a TVET certified International Skills Master Trainer.

Ahmed Jibrin Ahmed is a Water Management and Governance expert with 10 years experience. During the course of his career he successfully served with a range of both international and local clients, including , Tecnoserve Nigeria. He is also a member of COREN
